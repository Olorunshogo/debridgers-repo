## Debridgers

Debridgers is a B2B agricultural supply chain marketplace focused on Nigerian farm produce (grains/beans like modu packs, staples, tubers, proteins, oils – evident from landing page images and backend pricing at ₦1,300/pack). It operates a consignment model (Mode 2):

1. **Debridgers sources stock** from farmers/suppliers (warehouse inventory tracking).
2. **Agents** (sales distributors) apply -> get admin-approved -> complete KYC -> request stock packs -> sell locally (markets/caterers) -> remit proceeds.
3. **Buyers** (businesses/end-users) purchase via agents or platform (future direct orders).
4. **Commissions automated**: direct sales %, buyer referrals (₦20/order), agent overrides (5% multi-level max depth 2), state managers (2%).
5. **Wallets track** owes/earnings in kobo (Paystack integration).
6. **Admin dashboard** manages approvals, KYC reviews, stock fulfillment, payouts.

Users: admins, agents, buyers, companies. Nigerian focus (Paystack, kobo, LGAs).

**Key role**:

- Agents: Field sales reps (Kaduna-focused initially), need LGA/address.
- Admins/Employees: Platform operations, approve agents/KYC, fulfill stock, payout commissions.
- Buyers: Businesses placing orders (with referral codes).
- Companies: Likely logistics/suppliers (roles enum).
- Farmers: Upstream suppliers (not yet coded, but implied via "farmers directly with buyers" mission).
- Logistics: Implied for delivery (stock dispatch, future rider app).
- Investors: Platform backers (not coded).
